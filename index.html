Understanding Female Body Shapes – Visualizer, Calculator & Measurement Guide

Welcome to the Female Body Shape Visualizer – a free, AI-powered tool that helps women identify, understand, and celebrate their unique body types. Our body shape calculator uses your bust, waist, and hip measurements to determine your natural silhouette — whether it's apple, pear, hourglass, spoon, rectangle, or triangle.

This website is designed for women who want to improve their style, confidence, and fit awareness. You’ll find detailed guides on body proportions, measurement charts, 3D visualization, and styling suggestions for every figure. Each body type page offers personalized fashion tips that make dressing for your shape easier and more fun.

With our interactive female body shape visualizer, you can explore how different body proportions appear in 2D and 3D form. Whether you’re learning how to measure your body correctly or searching for figure-friendly clothing ideas, our goal is to empower every woman to feel confident in her natural shape.

Popular Body Shape Types

  • Apple Body Shape – Broad shoulders, fuller bust, and slim legs.
  • Pear Body Shape – Narrow shoulders with wider hips.
  • Hourglass Body Shape – Balanced bust and hips with defined waist.
  • Rectangle Body Shape – Straight silhouette with minimal curves.
  • Spoon Body Shape – Slightly larger hips and gentle curves.
  • Triangle & X-Shape Body – Athletic and balanced proportions.

Our body shape measurement tool supports both inches and centimeters, ensuring precision and convenience worldwide. Use it today to discover your natural body type and learn how to style it with confidence!
