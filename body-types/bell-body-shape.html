Bell Body Shape

The Bell body shape is characterized by hips that are the widest part of the body, with a smaller bust and a defined waist. This creates a 'bell' like silhouette. It is very similar to the Pear and Triangle body shapes.

Key Traits

  • Hips are the widest part of your body.
  • Shoulders are narrower than the hips.
  • A well-defined waist.
  • You tend to gain weight on your hips and thighs first.
  • Smaller bust and upper body compared to the lower body.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Add volume to your upper body to create a more balanced look.
  • Wear boat neck tops, and tops with ruffles or other embellishments.
  • A-line skirts and dresses are very flattering.
  • Avoid clingy fabrics on your lower half.
  • Focus on upper body strength training to build up your shoulders and back.

Bell Body Shape – Elegant Lower Curve with Defined Waist

The Bell Body Shape is a graceful silhouette that closely resembles the pear or spoon type but with a more pronounced lower-body fullness and a gentle curve at the waist and hips. It reflects feminine balance and natural symmetry.

Our Female Body Type Calculator helps identify the bell shape by comparing bust, waist, and hip ratios. The 3D Body Visualizer displays a realistic model showing your unique body distribution.

Characteristics of Bell Body Type

  • Full hips and thighs create a bell-like lower half.
  • Waist is clearly defined and narrower than bust or hips.
  • Bust may appear smaller in comparison to the hips.
  • Weight gain occurs mostly in the lower region.

Fashion & Styling Tips for Bell Body Shape

  • Structured tops and padded shoulders balance the figure.
  • Use V-necks and statement necklaces to draw attention upward.
  • Bootcut and straight-leg trousers balance fuller hips.
  • Choose A-line skirts or fit-and-flare dresses for proportion.
  • Monochrome outfits elongate the body visually.

The bell shape highlights femininity and grace, offering a naturally curvy appeal. Styling with structure and symmetry ensures a confident, well-proportioned look.
