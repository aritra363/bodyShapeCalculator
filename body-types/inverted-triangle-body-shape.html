Inverted Triangle Body Shape

The Inverted Triangle body shape is characterized by shoulders that are broader than the hips. Women with this body type tend to have a larger bust and a wider back, with a less defined waist and narrower hips.

Key Traits

  • Shoulders are the widest part of your body.
  • Hips are narrower than the shoulders.
  • The waist may not be well-defined.
  • You have a larger bust and a wider back.
  • Legs are often long and slender.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Balance your upper body by adding volume to your lower body.
  • Wear A-line skirts and wide-leg trousers.
  • Avoid tops with spaghetti straps or boat necks that widen the shoulder area.
  • V-necks and halter tops can be flattering.
  • Focus on lower body strength training to build up your hips and thighs.

Inverted Triangle Body Shape – Strong Shoulders & Athletic Elegance

The Inverted Triangle Body Shape is identified by broad shoulders, a wider bust, and narrower hips. This creates a distinctive V-shaped silhouette that exudes strength, balance, and athletic appeal. Common among swimmers, models, and athletes, this body type appears naturally powerful and sculpted.

You can determine if you have an inverted triangle figure using our Female Body Shape Calculator and 3D Body Visualizer, which measure your bust-to-hip ratio and simulate your unique structure for accurate styling suggestions.

Key Traits of Inverted Triangle Shape

  • Shoulders and bust are broader than hips.
  • Waist may appear less defined or straight.
  • Hips and legs are slender and proportionate.
  • Upper body gives an athletic and upright posture.

Best Fashion Choices for Inverted Triangle Body Type

  • Choose V-necklines and wrap tops to soften broad shoulders.
  • Flared skirts and wide-leg trousers add visual balance to the hips.
  • Use darker shades for tops and lighter tones for bottoms to equalize form.
  • Soft fabrics and curved hemlines enhance a feminine appearance.
  • Avoid shoulder pads or bulky sleeves that over-emphasize the top.

The inverted triangle shape embodies confidence and poise. With smart styling, this body type achieves a perfectly balanced and elegant figure that highlights both athleticism and femininity.
