Rectangle Body Shape

The Rectangle body shape, also known as the Straight or Banana body type, describes individuals with a straight silhouette where the bust, waist, and hips are nearly the same width. This shape often gives an athletic, lean appearance with minimal waist definition.

Key Traits

  • Shoulders, waist, and hips are roughly the same width.
  • Little natural waist definition.
  • Straight or athletic appearance.
  • Even body fat distribution.
  • Often has a toned or lean look.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Use clothing to create curves — belted dresses, peplum tops, and wrap silhouettes work best.
  • Choose high-waisted bottoms to define the waistline.
  • Layering adds volume and balance.
  • Opt for structured jackets and flared skirts.
  • Incorporate resistance training to shape glutes and shoulders for a balanced look.

Rectangle Body Shape – Definition, Measurements & Styling Guide

The Rectangle Body Shape (also known as the straight or athletic body shape) is characterized by a balanced silhouette with minimal curves. The bust, waist, and hips are nearly the same width, resulting in a straight or rectangular outline. This body type is admired for its clean lines and versatility in styling.

Using our Female Body Shape Calculator & 3D Visualizer, you can quickly determine if you have a rectangular body shape by entering your bust, waist, and hip measurements. Instantly visualize your figure and receive personalized fashion tips to enhance your proportions.

Key Traits of Rectangle Body Shape

  • Bust, waist, and hips have similar measurements.
  • Waistline is not distinctly defined.
  • Shoulders and hips are evenly aligned.
  • Weight is evenly distributed across the body.

Styling Tips for Rectangle Body Type

For women with a rectangle or athletic figure, the goal is to create the illusion of curves and add definition to the waist. Outfits that emphasize the bust and hips while cinching the waistline help balance the overall look.

  • Opt for belted dresses, wrap tops, and peplum blouses to highlight your waist.
  • Try flared skirts, bootcut jeans, or ruffled tops to add volume and femininity.
  • Layering with fitted blazers or cropped jackets enhances body proportions.
  • Choose textured or patterned fabrics to add depth and movement.
  • Highlight your shoulders with halter or off-shoulder designs.

Body Type Confidence & Styling Balance

The Rectangle body type exudes a naturally athletic and balanced appearance. With the right outfits and styling techniques, you can enhance your figure by defining curves and accentuating your waist. Embrace your proportions — your strength lies in structure, symmetry, and elegance.
