Rectangle Body Shape

The Rectangle body shape, also known as the Straight or Banana body type, describes individuals with a straight silhouette where the bust, waist, and hips are nearly the same width. This shape often gives an athletic, lean appearance with minimal waist definition.

Key Traits

  • Shoulders, waist, and hips are roughly the same width.
  • Little natural waist definition.
  • Straight or athletic appearance.
  • Even body fat distribution.
  • Often has a toned or lean look.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Use clothing to create curves — belted dresses, peplum tops, and wrap silhouettes work best.
  • Choose high-waisted bottoms to define the waistline.
  • Layering adds volume and balance.
  • Opt for structured jackets and flared skirts.
  • Incorporate resistance training to shape glutes and shoulders for a balanced look.
