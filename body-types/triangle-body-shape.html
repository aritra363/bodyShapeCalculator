Triangle Body Shape

The Triangle body shape, also known as the Pear body shape, is characterized by hips that are wider than the bust and shoulders. Women with this body type tend to have a smaller upper body and carry more weight on their hips, thighs, and bottom.Click here to download

Key Traits

  • Hips are the widest part of your body.
  • Shoulders are narrower than the hips.
  • A well-defined waist.
  • You tend to gain weight on your hips and thighs first.
  • Smaller bust and upper body compared to the lower body.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Balance your figure by adding volume to your upper body.
  • Wear patterned or brightly colored tops and dark-colored bottoms.
  • A-line skirts and wide-leg trousers are very flattering.
  • Use push-up bras or padded tops to enhance your bust.
  • Focus on upper body strength training to build up your shoulders and back.

Characteristics of Triangle Body Shape

  • Hips are significantly wider than bust and shoulders.
  • Waistline is well defined and naturally narrow.
  • Weight tends to accumulate in the hips, thighs, and lower region.
  • Upper body appears smaller compared to lower half.

Styling Tips for Triangle Body Type

  • Use structured tops, puff sleeves, and boat necklines to balance proportions.
  • Choose A-line skirts and straight-cut pants to elongate the figure.
  • Experiment with light or patterned tops and darker bottoms for balance.
  • Layered jackets or shoulder-enhancing blazers add symmetry.
  • Highlight your waist with belts or wrap dresses for a defined look.
