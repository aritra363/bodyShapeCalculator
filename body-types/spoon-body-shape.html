Spoon Body Shape

The Spoon body shape is similar to the Pear shape but with a more defined waist and a 'shelf' or 'saddlebag' area on the hips. Women with this body type have hips that are larger than their bust and carry weight in their hips and upper thighs.

Key Traits

  • Hips are wider than the bust.
  • A well-defined waist.
  • You may have a 'shelf' on your hips.
  • Weight is primarily stored in the hips and thighs.
  • Smaller upper body.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Balance your upper body with your lower body.
  • Wear tops with details like ruffles or patterns to draw attention upward.
  • Bootcut and flared jeans are flattering.
  • A-line dresses and skirts work well.
  • Focus on exercises that build upper body strength and tone the lower body.

Spoon Body Shape – Definition, Characteristics & Styling Ideas

The Spoon Body Shape (also known as the pear-top or bottom-heavy figure) is one of the most feminine and curvy body types. It is characterized by fuller hips and thighs, a defined waist, and a slightly smaller bust and shoulders. Women with this body type naturally have a graceful lower-body dominance that creates a beautiful spoon-like silhouette.

The tool instantly generates a realistic 3D visualization of your proportions and provides custom fashion recommendations tailored for the spoon body shape.

Key Features of Spoon Body Type

  • Hips are noticeably wider than the bust and shoulders.
  • Waist is well-defined and curves inward beautifully.
  • Weight tends to accumulate around the lower belly, hips, or thighs.
  • Shoulders are narrower compared to the lower half.

Styling Tips for Spoon Body Shape

The goal for spoon-shaped women is to balance the upper and lower body by drawing attention upward and defining the waist. Structured tops and layered outfits help achieve a more symmetrical look.

  • Wear off-shoulder tops, boat necklines, or ruffled blouses to broaden the shoulders.
  • Opt for A-line skirts, high-waisted jeans, or straight-leg trousers to elongate the legs.
  • Use belts and wrap dresses to highlight the waistline.
  • Choose darker shades for bottoms and brighter or printed tops for contrast.
  • Layer with fitted jackets or blazers to create vertical balance.

Confidence & Body Harmony

The Spoon body shape represents natural femininity and balance. When styled strategically, it enhances your curves while maintaining proportion and flow. With the right fabrics and silhouettes, spoon-shaped women can effortlessly highlight their best features and achieve a graceful, confident appearance.
