Pear Body Shape

The Pear body shape, also known as the triangle, is characterized by hips that are significantly wider than the bust and shoulders. Women with this body type tend to have a smaller upper body and carry more weight on their hips, thighs, and bottom.

Key Traits

  • Hips are the widest part of your body.
  • Shoulders are narrower than the hips.
  • A well-defined waist.
  • You tend to gain weight on your hips and thighs first.
  • Smaller bust and upper body compared to the lower body.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Balance your figure by adding volume to your upper body.
  • Wear patterned or brightly colored tops and dark-colored bottoms.
  • A-line skirts and wide-leg trousers are very flattering.
  • Use push-up bras or padded tops to enhance your bust.
  • Focus on upper body strength training to build up your shoulders and back.

Pear Body Shape – Styling Guide, Features & Measurement Insights

The Pear Body Shape — also known as the triangle body shape — features wider hips and thighs with a smaller bust and defined waist. Women with this figure have naturally feminine curves, giving them a balanced and graceful look when dressed according to their proportions.

Our Female Body Shape Calculator helps you identify if you have a pear-shaped figure using your bust, waist, and hip measurements. The integrated 3D body shape visualizer instantly displays your body structure, providing you with deeper insights into styling choices and fit recommendations.

If you’re pear-shaped, the key to styling is balancing your proportions. Choose tops that add volume to your shoulders and bust area — like boat necklines, puff sleeves, or layered tops — while keeping bottoms simple and structured. A-line skirts, straight-cut pants, and darker tones on the lower half will flatter your figure.

Style & Fashion Tips for Pear Body Shape

  • Highlight your waistline with fitted belts or wrap dresses.
  • Wear bright or patterned tops to draw attention upward.
  • Opt for wide-leg trousers or A-line skirts for balance.
  • Avoid clingy fabrics around the hips; instead, use flowy materials.
  • Layer jackets or blazers to add proportion to your shoulders.

The Pear body type is one of the most common and classically feminine shapes. With the right fashion strategy, you can create harmony between your upper and lower body — enhancing your curves while maintaining a sleek silhouette.
