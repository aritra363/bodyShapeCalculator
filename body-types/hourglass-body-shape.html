Hourglass Body Shape

The Hourglass body shape is characterized by a well-defined waist with bust and hip measurements that are roughly even. This classic, curvy silhouette is often considered one of the most balanced body types.Click here to download

Key Traits

  • Bust and hips are well-balanced and roughly the same size.
  • A clearly defined, narrow waist.
  • Shoulders are slightly rounded and align with the hips.
  • Body fat is evenly distributed in both the upper and lower body.
  • The body shape resembles the shape of an hourglass.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Emphasize your waist with belts and high-waisted clothing.
  • Wear form-fitting and tailored silhouettes like wrap dresses and pencil skirts.
  • V-necks and sweetheart necklines complement your bust.
  • Avoid boxy or shapeless clothing that hides your waist.
  • Focus on full-body workouts that tone your entire body evenly to maintain your natural proportions.

Key Features of Hourglass Body Shape

  • Waistline is 8–12 inches smaller than bust and hips.
  • Shoulders and hips align for a perfectly balanced silhouette.
  • Weight is evenly distributed throughout the body.
