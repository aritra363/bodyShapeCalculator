Apple Body Shape

The Apple body shape describes individuals with broader shoulders and bust than hips. Typically, this body type carries weight around the midsection with a less defined waistline, while legs remain slender and well-proportioned.

Key Traits

  • Broader shoulders and bust compared to hips.
  • Waist is less defined.
  • Often carries weight around the abdomen.
  • Legs are typically lean and toned.
  • Upper body appears more dominant than the lower body.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Focus on V-necks, open collars, and structured tops.
  • Empire waistlines elongate the torso and balance proportions.
  • A-line skirts and bootcut pants add lower-body harmony.
  • Avoid clingy tops; go for draped fabrics.
  • Engage in core-strengthening workouts to define the waistline.

Apple Body Shape – Complete Style, Measurement & Visual Guide

The Apple Body Shape is characterized by a fuller upper body — broad shoulders, prominent bust, and a less defined waistline. Women with this body type often have slender legs and arms, making their silhouette naturally elegant and proportionate when styled correctly.

At Female Body Shape Visualizer, our Apple body shape calculator uses your bust, waist, and hip measurements to identify your figure type accurately. This helps you understand your proportions and visualize your unique body in 3D form using our advanced body shape visualizer tool.

Dressing for the Apple body type means focusing on balance. Choose V-neck tops, empire-line dresses, A-line skirts, and high-waisted trousers to draw attention away from the midsection and highlight your legs and neckline. Lightweight fabrics and vertical patterns help create an elongated look.

Styling Tips for Apple Body Shape

  • Opt for structured jackets to define your shoulders and waistline.
  • Wear darker tops with lighter bottoms to balance proportions.
  • Avoid clingy fabrics around the waist; instead, choose draped styles.
  • Show off your legs with knee-length skirts or slim trousers.
  • Accessorize with long necklaces to visually elongate your torso.

The Apple body shape is beautiful and confident — a symbol of strength, balance, and poise. With the right knowledge and outfits, you can highlight your best features and achieve timeless style harmony.
