Banana Body Shape

The Banana body shape, also known as the Rectangle body type, describes individuals with a straight silhouette where the bust, waist, and hips are nearly the same width. This shape often gives an athletic, lean appearance with minimal waist definition.

Key Traits

  • Shoulders, waist, and hips are roughly the same width.
  • Little natural waist definition.
  • Straight or athletic appearance.
  • Even body fat distribution.
  • Often has a toned or lean look.

Style & Fitness Tips

  • Use clothing to create curves — belted dresses, peplum tops, and wrap silhouettes work best.
  • Choose high-waisted bottoms to define the waistline.
  • Layering adds volume and balance.
  • Opt for structured jackets and flared skirts.
  • Incorporate resistance training to shape glutes and shoulders for a balanced look.

Banana Body Shape – Balanced, Athletic & Straight Silhouette

The Banana Body Shape (also known as the rectangle or athletic body type) is characterized by an evenly distributed figure, where the bust, waist, and hips are nearly the same width. This gives a straight, balanced silhouette that is both versatile and easy to style for all fashion types.

Using our Body Shape Calculator & 3D Visualizer, you can measure your proportions and instantly see a digital version of your banana figure to help identify your best styling options and fashion fits.

Key Traits of the Banana Body Type

  • Shoulders, waist, and hips have similar measurements.
  • Body appears straight with fewer natural curves.
  • Weight is evenly distributed across the body.
  • Toned arms and legs give an athletic appearance.

Best Outfits for Banana Body Shape

  • Peplum tops and belted dresses create a curvier waistline illusion.
  • Use ruffled blouses, off-shoulder tops, or wrap dresses to add dimension.
  • High-waisted jeans and flared pants balance the figure elegantly.
  • Experiment with layered clothing to build visual curves.
  • Opt for contrasting colors to define the waist visually.

The banana shape embodies natural symmetry and a modern, sporty aesthetic. With a few styling tweaks, this body type can easily mimic curves and achieve a sophisticated yet relaxed look.
