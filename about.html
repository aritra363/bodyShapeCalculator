About This Project

The Female Body Shape Visualizer is a free, open-source tool designed to help women understand their body proportions and identify their body shape. By inputting measurements, users can generate a 3D model that visually represents their figure, along with a classification into common body types like Apple, Pear, Hourglass, and more.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide an accessible and intuitive tool that promotes body positivity and self-understanding. We believe that knowing your body shape is the first step toward dressing with confidence and developing effective fitness plans.

Technology

This project is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The 3D visualization is powered by the Three.js library, which allows for dynamic rendering of the female body model based on user inputs.
