How to Measure Your Body Shape Correctly

Understanding your body shape starts with accurate measurements. This guide will help you take your bust, waist, and hip measurements correctly — all you need is a measuring tape and a mirror.

1. Measuring Your Bust

Wrap the tape measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping it straight across your back. Don’t pull too tight — it should rest comfortably against your skin.

2. Measuring Your Waist

Find your natural waistline, which is usually the narrowest part of your torso (just above your belly button). Breathe normally and keep the tape snug but not tight.

3. Measuring Your Hips

Measure around the widest part of your hips and buttocks. Stand with your feet together to get an accurate reading.
